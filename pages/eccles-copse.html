Eccles Copse

Eccles Copse is the spiritual home and memory-place of Longspooning.

The Archive records. Eccles remembers.

The Place

Eccles is not merely another Order, nor merely another woodland. It is the ground of the highest Championship and the place to which uncertain memory is eventually drawn.

The Eccles Spoon

When you play with an Eccles Spoon, you are handling the shaft of a past champion — handle it deftly.

The Keepers

The Keepers of the Copse hold the knowledge where the written rule fails. They are recognised by the Association. They do not require the Association’s permission.
