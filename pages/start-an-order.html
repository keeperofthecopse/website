Start an Order

Any gathering of longspooners may call itself an Order. This is not the same as being remembered.

Before Declaring

Those wishing to declare an Order should first consider whether they have a place to gather, a longspoon worthy of use, a Field willing to witness, a custom worth preserving, a tolerance for mockery, and a reason to be remembered.

What an Order Needs

  • Woodland, canopy, fire and darkness for true play
  • A longspoon
  • A morsel worthy of challenge
  • Witnesses
  • A willingness to record champions and shame

Submission

The Archive may receive Order names, founding claims, bout accounts, champion records, titles and shames.

Submission is not recognition. Recognition is earned around the fire.
