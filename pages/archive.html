The Archive

The Archive is the public record of Longspooning.

It records what can be written down without pretending that writing is the same as truth.

What the Archive Records

  • Bout accounts
  • Champions
  • Titles
  • Shames
  • Orders
  • Public Notices
  • Disputes
  • Lost customs
  • Corrections and objections

What the Archive Does Not Do

The Archive does not create Orders, appoint champions, overturn the Field, rehear disputes, measure shafts, certify truth, or remove shame because it has become inconvenient.

Imperfect by Nature

The Archive is incomplete. Some accounts are missing. Some are contradictory. Some were submitted too late, too proudly, or by men with reason to improve their memory.

A perfect record would be suspicious.

The Authority of the Archive

The Field judges.

The Archive records.

Eccles remembers.
