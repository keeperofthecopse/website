Submit to the Archive

The Archive receives records. It is not obliged to believe them.

Submission Types

  • Bout account
  • Order record
  • Title or shame
  • Notice request
  • Correction or objection

What to Include

Where gathered, who stood, what morsel was chosen, who prevailed, what shame occurred, and what the Field remembers.

Standing Warning

A correction must offer more than wounded pride. An objection must be more than the complaint of a man who dropped the morsel.
