<p class="p1"># Longspooning Archive — Design Principles</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Core Principle</p>
<p class="p1">The site should feel like an archive, not a product.</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Tone</p>
<p class="p1">- restrained</p>
<p class="p1">- text-first</p>
<p class="p1">- slightly institutional</p>
<p class="p1">- not modern or stylised</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Layout</p>
<p class="p1">- mobile-first</p>
<p class="p1">- vertical flow</p>
<p class="p1">- no card-based UI</p>
<p class="p1">- no dashboard patterns</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Typography</p>
<p class="p1">- serif for content</p>
<p class="p1">- sans-serif for UI</p>
<p class="p1">- strong hierarchy</p>
<p class="p1">- high readability</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Colour</p>
<p class="p1">- off-white background</p>
<p class="p1">- near-black text</p>
<p class="p1">- green for links</p>
<p class="p1">- brown for subtle structure only</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Containment</p>
<p class="p1">- use background tone, not borders</p>
<p class="p1">- avoid boxes and cards</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Interaction</p>
<p class="p1">- minimal</p>
<p class="p1">- no animations</p>
<p class="p1">- no app-like behaviour</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Authority</p>
<p class="p1">- the Field &gt; Archive &gt; Eccles</p>
<p class="p1">- design should reinforce this subtly</p>
<p class="p2"><br></p>
<p class="p1">## Rule</p>
<p class="p1">If a design choice makes the site feel like a modern web product, reject it.</p>
